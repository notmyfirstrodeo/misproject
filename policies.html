Financing

We believe in making our sustainable products accessible to everyone. To help ease the financial commitment, we offer several flexible payment options to fit your budget. Choose from standard credit card payments, interest-free installment services, or trusted third-party financing partners that let you pay over time without added stress. If you need more information on how to take advantage of these payment methods, please reach out to our team. Our goal is to ensure that adopting eco-friendly solutions doesn’t feel like a financial hurdle.

Delivery

All products are delivered using eco-friendly packaging. We carefully select our packaging materials to ensure minimal environmental impact. We use recyclable or compostable materials whenever possible and avoid excess waste. Most orders ship within 2-3 business days, and tracking information will be provided once your order leaves our facility. We are committed to working with responsible shipping partners who share our values in sustainability and efficiency.

Returns

Returns are accepted within 30 days. If you’re not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a refund or exchange within 30 days of delivery. Simply contact us to initiate the return process. We’ll provide a prepaid return label and instructions. To qualify for a return, products must be in their original, unused condition. Once we receive and inspect your returned items, we’ll process your refund or exchange promptly.
